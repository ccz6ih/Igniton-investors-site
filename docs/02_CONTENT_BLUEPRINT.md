## PORTFOLIO (`/#portfolio`) — 4 SKUs as revenue assets, NO price/cart/subscribe

Grid of 4 cards. Each: name + one-line positioning + correctly-tiered study claim + exact figures + citation + `**`. Note the **Cognition + Longevity** pairing as the validated top revenue line.

### _Igni_Cognition™ (🟢) — tier: *university study, peer-reviewed journal*
- Positioning: "Sharper memory, stronger focus, faster thinking — measurable in 30 days.**"
- Figures: **+83%** quality of mental performance · **+51%** sustained attention · **+28%** short-term memory · **+25%** operational memory.**
- Citation: Valverde R. et al., 2023 — "Influence of Enhanced Amino Acid Compositions on Human Cognitive Functioning," HSOA Journal of Alternative, Complementary & Integrative Medicine (Concordia University). 90-day RCT, 80 subjects, 3 arms incl. placebo.

### _Igni_Longevity™ (🟢) — tier: *university study, peer-reviewed journal*
- Positioning: "Less discomfort, faster recovery, feeling better — measurable in 30 days.**"
- Figures: **−37%** IL-6 · **−28%** CRP · **−13%** GGT · **−8/9%** blood pressure.** ("Same compounds. Same dose. Different outcome. The variable was the enhancement.")
- Citation: Int'l Journal of Studies in Psychology, DOI 10.38140/ijspsy.v5i1.1616. 30-day RCT, 70 subjects, 3 arms, 3 independent measurement methods.

### _Igni_Peptide Eye Serum™ (🟢) — tier: *clinical study*
- Positioning: "An eye treatment engineered to work where most products quit — Igniton-charged EGF."
- Figures: **+52%** hydration · **+30%** barrier function (24h) · **100%** of subjects improved; the charged formula performed **34% better** than the same non-charged ingredients on barrier.*
- Citation: 8-week independent clinical trial, ALS Global (CRO), 52 healthy female subjects.

### _Igni_REM Sleep™ (🟢) — tier: *a study* (initial, non-published)
- Positioning: "Brighter mornings, more rested — more REM sleep measurable in 30 days. No melatonin."
- Figures: **+25%** more REM vs placebo · fewer wake-ups (2.6→1.2/night) · more total sleep (6.3→7.1 hrs).**
- Citation: open randomized enhanced-vs-placebo trial, 14 days vs 7-day baseline, 60 healthy people aged 40–76.
